Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management

Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management (BSTM) is a degree program that delivers education to people who are interested to build a career
in the travel and leisure industry involving tourism planning, development, implementation and control management functions. The primary goal
of BSTM program is to produce competent, values br driven and service-oriented professionals by providing the required knowledge, skills and
values that will enable them to become globally competitive professionals.
